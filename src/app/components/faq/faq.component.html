Frequently asked questions
What types of games are available at the game bar?

We offer a wide variety of games, including the latest video game titles on top-tier consoles, immersive virtual reality experiences, and a selection of classic and modern board games. There's something for everyone, whether you're a casual gamer or a hardcore enthusiast.

Do I need to make a reservation to visit the game bar?

Reservations are not required for general entry, but they are highly recommended for larger groups or during peak hours to ensure availability. You can easily book your spot online or by giving us a call.

Are food and drinks available at the game bar?

Absolutely! Our game bar features a delicious menu of snacks, meals, and beverages, including non-alcoholic options and cocktails. You can enjoy your food and drinks while you play.

Can I host private events or parties at the game bar?

Yes! We offer private game rooms and event packages for birthdays, corporate gatherings, and other special occasions. Contact us to customize your event and make it a memorable gaming experience.
