Frequently asked questions
What types of games are available at the game bar?

While you’re always welcome to walk in, we highly recommend booking a spot in advance, especially if you're coming with a group or during peak hours. That way, you’ll guarantee your place in the action! Reservations are quick and easy to make online, or feel free to give us a call – we’ll make sure your spot is ready for you!

How do I book a private room?

Booking your own private game room is easy and hassle-free. Simply visit our website, choose the room and time that fits your schedule, and book online in just a few clicks. Need help or have questions? Give us a call, and our friendly staff will assist you with every detail to ensure your room is set up exactly the way you want it!

Our Game Bar is designed for gamers of all ages! However, certain games and areas (like VR or our alcohol menu) may have age restrictions. Please check with us when you book, and we'll make sure you're all set to enjoy the experience to the fullest.
